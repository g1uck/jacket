Style

Transformable oval polycarbonate protective lens to protect against environmental influences and keep your privacy, which is hidden in the hood comes in two variations — clear and mirrored (supports Face ID). Security pocket with Faraday grid lining ​​makes the device inside invisible and protects your cards from the scamming, as it cancels all electronic components outside the pocket.

Composition

The hood, with snap-fastened strap at neck, can be split in two halves, through a zipper hidden under an opaque nylon fabric, held by snaps. Two slanting pockets with overstitched flap and hidden zipper fastening on front. Opaque nylon patch on elbows. Ribbed cuffs. Two-way zipper fastening edged with tapes, hidden by the overstitched placket with buttons held by elasticized loops.

Care

Wash at max 30°C - Mild cycle; Do not bleach; Tumble dry at low heat; Ironing maximum temperature 110°C - without steam; Do not dry clean; Line dry in the shade; Washing can alter color intensity, wash inside out, wash separately; Use neutral detergent; Remove detachable accessories before washing; Iron with a cloth between the item and the iron
